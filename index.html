About me

I am a Fisheries Development Officer based in Nepal, focused on aquaculture development, farmer extension, and sustainable management of freshwater resources. I work across community ponds, hatcheries, and cooperative groups to improve productivity while safeguarding biodiversity and water quality.

My work blends technical advisory with hands-on field support—from pond design and seed stocking plans to feed budgeting, health management, and market linkage. I collaborate with local governments and NGOs to implement inclusive programs that raise household income and food security.
